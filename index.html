BUDGET CALCULATOR

This is a budget calculator that can be used add up money coming in and out. You can use it to see where your money is going or to plan your budget. To use it, simply enter the name of the cashflow item and its value in the designated field, then click add. Expenses require a (-) before the amount. You can update or delete a cashflow item at any time. You must be signed-in to use the calculator and only you can see your cashflow list. Your work will also be saved for you automatically.
